Full time job

Key Responsibilities Handle inbound customer calls promptly and efficiently. Make outbound calls for follow-ups, surveys, or promotional campaigns. Respond to customer queries via phone, email, or live chat. Provide accurate information about products, services, or bookings. Resolve complaints or issues professionally and courteously. Log and maintain detailed records of customer interactions. Escalate complex issues to supervisors or relevant departments. Follow call centre scripts and company policies while maintaining a personal touch. Meet key performance indicators (KPIs) such as call handling time, resolution rates, and customer satisfaction. Participate in training sessions and team meetings to improve service quality.

Requirements & Qualifications Excellent communication and interpersonal skills. Strong command of English (spoken and written). Previous experience in customer service or call centre environment preferred. Ability to handle high call volumes professionally. Good problem-solving and conflict resolution skills. Basic computer skills, including MS Office and CRM software. Ability to work flexible shifts, including evenings or weekends if required. Desirable Skills Experience with CRM platforms such as Salesforce or Zoho. Multilingual abilities to assist diverse customers. Knowledge of UK consumer rights and regulations. Patience, empathy, and ability to remain calm under pressure.
